# USDC

- **Description:** USD-backed stablecoin; maintains 1:1 parity with US Dollar
- **Status:** Active
- **Maintained by:** Circle (primary issuer)
- **Key Networks:** Ethereum, Stellar, other blockchain platforms

## Use Cases
- Remittances on blockchain networks (especially Stellar)
- DeFi collateral and trading
- Cross-border settlement alternative to traditional payment rails
