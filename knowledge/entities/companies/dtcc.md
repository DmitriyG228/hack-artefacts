# DTCC

- **Full Name:** Depository Trust & Clearing Corporation
- **Description:** Core financial infrastructure provider for US securities markets; primary custodian of US equities; operates settlement and clearing infrastructure
- **Industry:** Financial Infrastructure / Securities Settlement
- **Headquarters:** New York, USA
- **Core Responsibility:** Custody and settlement of 100+ trillion in US equities
- **Unique Governance:** Owned by members; governed for benefit of industry rather than commercial profit

## Mission
Provide trust infrastructure enabling financial markets to operate. Custodies majority (if not entirety) of US equities market assets.

## Involvement in Blockchain & Tokenization
- **Start Date:** January 2016
- **Strategy:** Multi-L1 tokenization of equities on Ethereum, Canton, and other networks
- **Goal:** Enable clients' digital assets journey without disrupting existing TradFi actors
- **Compliance Approach:** Maintain KYC/AML perimeter; no bearer instruments; wallet transfers permitted only between registered wallets
- **Business Model:** Bridge TradFi and DeFi; enable DeFi protocols to participate as direct members or through existing ~300 direct participants
