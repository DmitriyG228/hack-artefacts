# Advanced AI Society

- **Description:** Association of builders working on "proof of control" technologies for the age of AI; focused on ensuring human agency and control in AI systems
- **Industry:** AI Governance / Trust Infrastructure
- **Leadership:** [[Michael Casey]], Chairman
- **Focus:** Ensuring enterprises and deployers can trust AI systems; addressing control vs. AI autonomy issues

## Mission
Build technical and governance frameworks ensuring human authority and control remain foundational in AI systems; prevent centralized control of AI by major tech companies.
