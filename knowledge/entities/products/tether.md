# Tether (USDT)

- **Description:** Largest and most widely used stablecoin, pegged to the US Dollar. Operates offshore (Cayman Islands) with controversial reserve backing.
- **Status:** Active / Dominant market position
- **Maintained by:** Tether Holdings Limited
