# Office of the Comptroller of the Currency (OCC)

- **Description:** US federal agency responsible for chartering, regulating, and supervising national banks
- **Industry:** Government Financial Regulation
- **Involvement:** Key role in stablecoin regulatory framework; issued interpretive letters supporting stablecoins in 2020-2021

## Key Actions
- **September 2020**: OCC interpretive letter on stablecoins
- **January 2021**: OCC interpretive letter supporting bank involvement with stablecoins
- These letters were pivotal in shifting narrative from prohibition toward structured enablement
