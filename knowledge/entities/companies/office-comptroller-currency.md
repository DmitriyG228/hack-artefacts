# Office of the Comptroller of the Currency (OCC)

- **Description:** Bureau of the US Department of the Treasury responsible for federal banking regulation and supervision of national banks and federal savings associations
- **Industry:** Federal Banking Regulation
- **Involvement:** Early supporter of stablecoin innovation; issued interpretive letters clarifying bank authority to work with stablecoin issuers

## Representatives
- Brian Brooks — Comptroller of the Currency

## Meeting Mentions
- 2024-05-17 — The US Government and Stablecoins: Referenced for 2020-2021 interpretive letters by Comptroller Brian Brooks supporting stablecoin innovation and clarifying that national banks could provide reserves and custody services for stablecoin issuers.
